College Management System

PDF-Ready Requirements Document

Document Type: Business Requirements / Functional Requirements
Project: College Management System (CMS)
Prepared For: Product, Engineering, Operations, Accounts
Version: 1.0

Purpose. This document defines the functional requirement scope for a real-world college management system with student lifecycle management, fees and accounts, online payments, communications, hostel-style movement workflows, library, inventory, shop/canteen, and reports.

1. Scope Overview

The system shall support the following major business domains:

  • Master setup and administration
  • Admissions and student registration
  • Student information management
  • Fees, invoices, payments, ledger, and accounts
  • Student and parent portal access
  • Circulars, schedules, notifications, and reminders
  • Leave, outpass, and check-in / check-out workflows
  • Inventory, expenses, library, shop, and canteen operations
  • Reports and audit-supporting outputs

2. Core Business Requirements

2.1 Master Setup and Administration
  • Institution profile setup
  • Academic year, semester, and session setup
  • Department, course, class, batch, and section setup
  • User role and permission management
  • Fee head and charge master setup
  • Notification, reminder, and system settings
2.2 Admissions Management
  • Admission enquiry and application intake
  • Document verification workflow
  • Provisional / temporary admission status
  • Final admission approval workflow
  • Automatic generation of unique registration number at confirmed admission stage
  • Advance payment capture during admission
  • Advance refund / adjustment options
2.3 Student Profile Management
  • Unique dynamic registration number
  • Personal details: name, gender, date of birth, phone, email, address, status
  • Parent / guardian details
  • Course / class / batch allocation
  • Admission date and student status tracking
  • Document and identity details if required by institution
  • Profile-wise visibility of fees, payment history, invoices, ledger, leave, outpass, and movement records
2.4 Fees Management System
  • Student-wise fee structure creation
  • Support custom fee heads such as tuition, transport, hostel, exam, and other institution-defined charges
  • Due date setup at invoice or installment level
  • Installment plan definition and tracking
  • Optional fine calculation rules
  • Scholarship, concession, waiver, and discount handling
  • Advance admission payment adjustment into final fee billing
  • Staff fee module excluded from scope
2.5 Invoices, Payments, and Ledger
  • Invoice creation for regular and advance-linked fee billing
  • Individual ledger for each student
  • Ledger to track billed fees, payments received, pending dues, and adjustments
  • Separate visibility of advance vs regular fee records
  • Payment receipt generation
  • Invoice PDF download
  • Payment status tracking: created, pending, success, failed, reversed if applicable
2.6 Online Payment Integration
  • Razorpay integration for student fee payments
  • Online payment initiation from student / parent portal
  • Server-side payment verification and auto confirmation
  • Auto update of invoice and student ledger after successful payment
  • Receipt and invoice download support
2.7 Student / Parent Portal
  • Login using student ID / registration number and phone number with OTP or password
  • Profile access
  • Fee dues and invoices
  • Payment history and ledger
  • Circulars and schedules
  • Leave request status
  • Outpass status
2.8 Circulars and Schedule Management
  • Admin publishing of circulars, announcements, exam schedules, and event schedules
  • Audience-based visibility for students, parents, and staff
  • Publish date and expiry date control
2.9 SMS, Email, and App Notifications
  • Fee due reminders
  • Payment confirmations
  • Leave approval / rejection alerts
  • Outpass approval / rejection alerts
  • Check-in / check-out alerts
  • Circular announcements
  • Support delivery through SMS, email, and in-app notifications
2.10 Leave Management
  • Workflow: Request → Approval / Denial → Status update
  • Request raised by parent
  • Approver actions: approve, deny, remarks
  • History and status tracking per student
2.11 Outpass Management
  • Request raised by parent
  • Approval by staff or admin
  • Track exit date/time, reason, expected return, actual return, and final status
2.12 Check-In / Check-Out
  • Movement tracking for hostel or gate use cases
  • Check-in and check-out event logging
  • Parent and staff notifications
  • Use cases include hostel entry/exit and school/college gate pass
2.13 Inventory Management
  • Academic inventory
  • Hostel inventory
  • General stock inventory
  • Purchase, usage, adjustment, and balance stock tracking
2.14 Expense Management
  • Institution expense entry
  • Category-wise tracking
  • Linking with accounts ledger or finance records
  • Expense reporting
2.15 Due Date Alerts
  • Before due date reminders
  • On due date reminders
  • Post-overdue reminders
  • Channels: SMS, email, app notification
2.16 Shop and Canteen Module
  • Separate login for shop admin and canteen operator
  • Student purchase billing
  • Credit / prepaid / wallet options as per institutional policy
  • Daily sales reporting
2.17 Canteen and Shop Inventory
  • Item stock management
  • Purchase and sales tracking
  • Automatic stock deduction on billing
2.18 Library Management System
  • Support one or more libraries, if required
  • Book catalog management
  • Issue / return workflow
  • Fine calculation for overdue returns
  • Optional linkage of fines to student ledger
2.19 Reports and Analytics
  • Student fee reports
  • Payment reports
  • Pending due reports
  • Inventory reports
  • Expense reports
  • Canteen / shop reports
  • Library reports
2.20 Common Platform Features
  • Global filters by date range, class / course, student name / registration number, payment status, due status
  • Search and pagination
  • Role-based access control
  • Audit-ready logs where required
  • Export support for PDF and Excel where applicable

3. Scope Exclusions

  • Staff fee processing is out of scope
  • Only student fee processing is in scope

4. Recommended Role Matrix

Role Primary Responsibility
Superadmin Full platform control, setup, users, settings, audit visibility
Admin Operational control of students, fees, circulars, reports, approvals
Admission Admission intake, verification, provisional/final admission processing
Accountant Fees, invoices, payments, student ledger, reports
Staff Leave/outpass approval, check-in/out monitoring, student operations
Librarian Library catalog, issue/return, fine processing
Student Portal access for profile, invoices, payments, ledger, circulars, status views
Parent Portal access, payments, leave requests, outpass requests, status tracking
Shop Admin / Canteen Operator Billing, stock, and daily sales management

5. Implementation Priority

  1. Master setup, admissions, student profile, fees, invoices, ledger, payments
  2. Student/parent portal and notifications
  3. Leave, outpass, check-in/out workflows
  4. Inventory, expense, library, shop/canteen modules
  5. Advanced reports, audit support, exports, analytics
